About iLatinHustleFest

Learn more about our festival, mission, and the team behind the magic

Our Story

iLatinHustleFest was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate Latin dance enthusiasts who wanted to create a festival that celebrates the rich diversity of Latin dance styles while fostering a supportive and inclusive community.

What started as a small gathering of 200 dancers has grown into one of the most anticipated Latin dance events in the country, attracting over 2,000 participants from around the world.

Our festival features world-class instructors, electrifying performances, and unforgettable social dancing experiences. We pride ourselves on creating an environment where dancers of all levels can learn, grow, and connect through their shared love of Latin dance.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide everything we do
