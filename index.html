About The Festival

Experience the passion and energy of Latin dance

A Celebration of Latin Dance

iLatinHustleFest brings together world-class instructors, performers, and dance enthusiasts for a weekend filled with workshops, social dancing, and unforgettable performances.

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced dancer, our festival offers classes for all levels in Salsa, Bachata, Kizomba, and more.
