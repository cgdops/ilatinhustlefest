The International Latin Hustle Festival FAQ's

Dive into a weekend of rhythm, fun, and new friends at the Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort.
From workshops to poolside parties, it's all here!

When's the e

Mark your calendars! The festival runs from August 21st to 24th, 2025. Get ready for a dance-packed weekend!

Where is the hotel?

Join us at the beautiful Westin Fort Lauderdale Beach Resort. Sun, sand, and dancing await!

What's in the VIP package?

Enjoy exclusive access to the Saturday gala dinner and all the weekend's festivities with our VIP package.

Are there beginner workshops?

Yes! We welcome all skill levels with workshops designed for beginners. Come join the fun!

How do I get tickets?

Grab your tickets on our website. Choose between VIP or regular passes and secure your spot now!

Is it just Latin Hustle?

Latin Hustle takes center stage, but we also offer salsa, bachata, cha cha cha, and many more!

Are there single day passes?

Single-day passes and evening social passes can be purchased on-site throughout the event.

Are the Intensive and Bootcamps included?

Billy's Latin Hustle Intensive and any additional bootcamps require an separate fee. Minimum of a weekend pass is required to participate.
