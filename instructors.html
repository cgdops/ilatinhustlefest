Carlos Rodriguez

Salsa & Bachata Instructor

Biography

Carlos Rodriguez is a world-renowned Salsa and Bachata instructor with over 15 years of experience in the Latin dance industry. Born and raised in Miami to Cuban parents, Carlos grew up immersed in Latin music and dance culture.

He began his professional dance career at the age of 18 and quickly rose to prominence in the international Latin dance scene. Carlos has performed and taught in over 30 countries, sharing his passion and knowledge with thousands of students worldwide.

His teaching style emphasizes proper technique, musicality, and connection with your partner. Carlos is known for his ability to break down complex movements into easy-to-understand components, making his classes accessible to dancers of all levels.

Achievements & Recognition

  • 5-time World Salsa Championship finalist
  • Featured instructor at the World Latin Dance Cup
  • Choreographer for several Latin music artists
  • Founder of the Miami Salsa Congress
  • Creator of the "Salsa Fundamentals" instructional video series
