Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about tickets and pricing

What's included in the registration fee?

Registration fees include access to workshops, parties, and events as specified in your selected pass. All passes include a festival wristband, program guide, and access to the vendor area. Food and accommodation are not included unless specifically mentioned.

Can I upgrade my pass during the festival?

Yes, you can upgrade your pass at the registration desk during the festival. You'll only need to pay the difference between your current pass and the upgraded pass. Please note that downgrades or refunds for unused portions of passes are not available.

Are there any discounts available?

We offer early bird discounts (typically 15% off) until two months before the festival. Group discounts are available for groups of 5+ (10% off) and 10+ (15% off). Students with valid ID receive a 10% discount on Basic and Full passes only.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available up to 30 days before the event with a 15% processing fee. Within 30 days of the event, no refunds are available, but you can transfer your pass to another person for a $25 transfer fee. In case of event cancellation, full refunds will be issued.

How do I select my workshops?

After purchasing your pass, you'll receive a confirmation email with instructions on how to select your workshops. Workshop selection opens one month before the festival and is on a first-come, first-served basis. VIP pass holders get priority workshop selection.
